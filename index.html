Why anonymous feedback matters

People are more honest when they're anonymous

Research consistently shows that people give more candid, useful feedback when their identity is protected. Whisperlist removes the social pressure so you get the truth — not what people think you want to hear.

Use cases

Team retrospectives where people can flag real issues. Product feedback from beta users. Honest friend polls for decisions. Event feedback without awkwardness. Management reviews where reports can speak freely.

Privacy by design

We don't track IP addresses, don't require accounts, and don't store identifying information. Responses are stored temporarily and auto-deleted after 30 days. Your whispers stay anonymous.
